Team photos from the first and second half of the excavation

There are a a lot of people I'd like to thank for helping me with this project, including my advisor Jason Herrmann and the director of the dig, Paola Sconzo, who generously allowed me to use not-yet-published data from the season. I had a great time on the excavation, and feel lucky to have met all the wonderful people working there. Everyone was welcoming, and making friends was not difficult. I look forward to seeing them again in the future. I'd also like to thank the Price Lab for awarding me the fellowship, which made creating this possible.


Citations:

Falsone, Gioacchino. 1981. Struttura e origine orietale dei forni da vasaio di Mozia. Palermo: Fondazione Giuseppe Whitaker.

Sconzo, Paola, and Gioacchino Falsone. “NEW INVESTIGATIONS IN THE NORTH-EAST QUARTER OF MOTYA. THE ARCHAIC CEMETERY AND BUILDING J (2017).” Folia Phoenicia 1 (2017): 62–69.
